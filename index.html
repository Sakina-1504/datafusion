FAQ

Before you install

Why does Windows show a blue "protected your PC" warning?

This app isn't digitally signed yet (that requires a paid certificate), so Windows shows this caution screen the first time any unsigned app runs — it isn't a sign of a problem. Click "More info", then "Run anyway" to continue.

What does the installer actually do?

It walks you through a short setup wizard: choose whether to add a desktop shortcut, then it installs the app and optionally launches it for you at the end. It also adds a normal "Uninstall" entry in Windows Settings if you ever want to remove it.

Does it need the internet to work?

No. Once installed, DataFusion Platform runs fully offline — your Excel files and reports never leave your computer.

Is it free?

Yes, completely free to download and use, no account or sign-up required.
